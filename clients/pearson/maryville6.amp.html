Why Choose Maryville?

  • History: Founded in 1872, Maryville is one of the oldest private institutions in the region.
  • Faculty: 90% of full-time Maryville faculty members hold Ph.D. or terminal degrees.
  • Personal Attention: Maryville has a student-to-faculty ratio of 13:1 and an average class size of 20.
  • Financial Assistance: Seek to qualify for financial aid.
